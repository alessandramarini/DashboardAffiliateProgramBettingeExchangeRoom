The Paradigm Shift

Expected Value vs Gambling

Traditional sports betting is mathematically designed to drain the user's bankroll over time. The bookmaker's margin (vigorish) guarantees a negative Expected Value (-EV) for the average punter, making long-term profitability statistically impossible without a quantitative edge.

Betting Exchange Room disrupts this model. By operating exclusively on betting exchanges, we eliminate the bookmaker's spread. We do not gamble; we execute algorithmic trades based on statistical anomalies and market inefficiencies.

The Old Way: Gambling

Emotional decisions, chasing losses, ignoring probability, fighting against mathematical disadvantages.

The New Way: Quantitative Trading

Strict bankroll management, compounding interest, finding +EV (Positive Expected Value) situations, emotionless execution.
